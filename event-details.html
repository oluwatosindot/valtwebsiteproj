VALT Financial Literacy Workshop

Join VALT for an interactive financial literacy workshop designed to equip high school learners with essential money management skills. This hands-on session covers budgeting fundamentals, the power of saving and compound interest, understanding debt, and building a healthy relationship with money. Led by seasoned entrepreneurs with over 18 years of experience in the financial industry, this workshop goes beyond textbook theory to deliver practical, real-world knowledge that learners can apply immediately.

Participants will engage in group activities, financial simulations, and guided discussions that make complex financial concepts accessible and exciting. Whether your learners are just starting to think about pocket money or already planning for their future careers, this workshop provides the foundation for a lifetime of smart financial decisions. Schools and community organisations are welcome to register groups. Space is limited, so early registration is encouraged.
