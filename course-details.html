VALT 101 - Emotional Intelligence & Identity


We help learners develop emotional mastery, social skills, and a strong sense of identity to enhance academic performance and lifelong wellbeing. This programme covers self-awareness, self-management, empathy, conflict resolution, positive affirmations, mental health, identity building, habit formation, and social embedding.
VALT 101 is designed for all high school learners. Whether you're in Grade 8 or Grade 12, this programme will help you develop essential emotional and social skills that will benefit you throughout your life.

  • Self-awareness & Self-management

  • Empathy & Social Skills

  • Conflict Resolution

  • Positive Affirmations & Mental Health

  • Identity Building

  • Habit Formation

  • Social Embedding

  • Academic Success Strategies
