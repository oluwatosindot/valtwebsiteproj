Who We Are

VALT is an educational enrichment provider offering future-ready learning experiences to high school learners. Our holistic programmes focus on Emotional Intelligence, Identity Development, Financial Literacy, and Entrepreneurship - equipping young people with the tools they need to thrive academically, socially, and professionally.


Founded in 2019 by a team of seasoned entrepreneurs who share a common vision, VALT has quickly established itself as a leading player in the financial literacy sector. Building strong partnerships with key industry players, the organisation has garnered recognition for its innovative approach to financial education. One of the founders brings over 18 years of valuable experience in the financial industry, underscoring VALT's commitment to uplifting and upskilling all South Africans as responsible stewards of their finances.


Notably, VALT has successfully collaborated with prestigious educational institutions such as Durban High School, Crawford, Curro and Cambridge International.

The Impact of Our Programme

Supporting students' holistic development beyond the classroom.

Academic Performance
Studies indicate that participation in our programmes often correlates with better academic performance, as students cultivate discipline and time management skills that enhance their studies.
Social & Emotional Growth
Our programs foster social and emotional growth, allowing students to form friendships, develop a sense of belonging, and connect with their school community. They also play a key role in building confidence and promoting physical and mental well-being.
Essential Life Skills
Students learn essential skills such as teamwork, leadership, time management, and problem-solving. Engaging in these activities encourages creative and critical thinking, equipping students to address real-world challenges effectively.
Future Prospects
Involvement in such programs can improve future prospects by presenting students as well-rounded candidates to colleges and employers. Overall, these initiatives contribute to a sense of community and can enhance the school's reputation.

Why Choose VALT?

VALT offers financial courses designed to empower young adults and adults with the knowledge and tools to manage their money and avoid future financial difficulties. Our interactive curriculum goes beyond basic math, teaching practical skills for lifelong financial success.
