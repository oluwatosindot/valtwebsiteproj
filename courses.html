Our Core Programmes

Equipping young people with the tools they need to thrive academically, socially, and professionally.

All Grades

VALT 101

Emotional Intelligence & Identity

We help learners develop emotional mastery, social skills, and a strong sense of identity to enhance academic performance and lifelong wellbeing.

Topics: Self-awareness, Self-management, Empathy, Conflict Resolution, Positive Affirmations, Mental Health, Identity Building, Habit Formation, Social Embedding

All Grades (Tax: Grades 11-12)

VALT 102

Financial Literacy

We transform teens into money-smart individuals by teaching practical tools for long-term financial independence.

Topics: Budgeting & Debt Management, Introduction to Investing & Wealth Creation, Personal Tax (Grades 11-12)

Grades 10-11

VALT 103

Internship Programme

Learners gain real-world experience through school holiday placements in partner businesses.

Topics: Workplace Exposure, Career Guidance, Skill Development

Grades 11-12

VALT 104

Entrepreneurship & Branding

We ignite entrepreneurial thinking and provide pathways to build meaningful personal and professional brands.

Topics: Business vs Employment Pathways, Startups Marketing Networking, Business & Personal Branding, Business Tax (Grades 11-12)

All Grades
